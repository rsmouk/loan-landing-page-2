Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Important: This extension is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with OpenAI, ChatGPT, or any related entities.

1. Overview

ChatGPT Search Results Extractor ("we," "our," or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension handles data when you use it.

2. Data Collection

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

2.1 Local Data Processing

The extension operates entirely locally within your browser. It temporarily processes the following information from ChatGPT API responses:

  • Search queries: The extension extracts search queries that ChatGPT uses when performing web searches
  • Chat IDs: Conversation identifiers to organize queries by conversation

This data is processed in-memory only and is never:

  • Sent to any external servers
  • Stored permanently on your device
  • Transmitted to third parties
  • Used for any purpose other than displaying search queries to you

2.2 Website Content Access

The extension accesses content from chatgpt.com only. It intercepts API responses to extract search query information. This access is necessary for the extension's core functionality.

3. Data Storage

No data is stored permanently. Search queries are kept in your browser's memory (RAM) only while the ChatGPT page is open. All data is automatically cleared when:

  • You close the ChatGPT tab
  • You refresh the page
  • You navigate away from chatgpt.com

4. Data Sharing and Transmission

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. All data processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. The extension does not communicate with any external servers or services.

5. Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • tabs: Used to open external links (support and developer links) in new browser tabs
  • chatgpt.com/*: Required to access ChatGPT pages and extract search queries from API responses

No other permissions are requested or used.

6. Third-Party Services

The extension does not integrate with or communicate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data collection services.

7. Children's Privacy

Our extension does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we do not collect any data at all, this is not applicable.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect, store, or process any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over the extension and can uninstall it at any time through Chrome's extension management.
